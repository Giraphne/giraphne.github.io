VL/HCC 2021

Online, October 10-13

VL/HCC is a conference on Visual Languages and Human-Centered Computing. It is a great fit for presenting our work on SQLVis, a visual representation for SQL. More information on our visit to this conference will follow soon.

Alice & Eve 2021

Nijmegen, the Netherlands, November 12

After a succesful first version in January 2020, and a postponement of the 2021 version, in November it is finally time for Alice & Eve. It is a workshop to celebrate women in computing in the Netherlands, and it is a good venue to meet other students, PhD candidates and other academics within the country. This network broadening is one of the reasons I enjoyed Alice and Eve 2020 so much. This year, I will have another poster to present some work under review.
