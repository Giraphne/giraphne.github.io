SIGMOD 2023

Seattle, USA, 18-23 June

We're so excited to announce that our DataEd workshop will be held in collaboration with SIGMOD again this year. We were very happy with last year's attendance (up to 40 people) and submissions (19). For our second iteration we are aiming to access an even wider network of people, also including the statistics education research community. DataEd '23 will take place on the last day of SIGMOD: June 23, 2023.
